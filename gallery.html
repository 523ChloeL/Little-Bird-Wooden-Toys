About Katie's Komets

"The Komets are a co-ed wheelchair basketball team for kids with physical disabilities. The organization is for kids in elementary school through high school graduation. The team was founded by Joe and Roseann Kirlin. They founded the Katie Kirlin Fund (the team’s main sponsor) in memory of their daughter, Katie Kirlin."
